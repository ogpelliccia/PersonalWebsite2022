Welcome to my webpage!

I am Nicholas Pelliccia, the creator of this web page. In general, I am quiet, creative, and as you can see from my webpage, one who stands out from the crowd and goes their own way. I first got into computers and programming at the age of 11 when I started playing around with virtualization, which is essentially running a computer inside of another computer. Over the years, I moved on to tinkering with real hardware, programming, and eventually, in the Fall semester of 2021, started going to LCCC to pursue an Associate's degree in Computer Information Systems (CIS). After graduating, I plan to transfer to a four-year college somewhere in this area and get my Bachelor's degree.
